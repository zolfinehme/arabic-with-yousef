Getting to Know You

Let us talk about you

Before we begin, I want to understand where you are coming from and where you wish to go.

I

Why do you want to learn Arabic?

Travel, faith, culture, business, or connecting with people? Understanding your reason shapes every lesson we share together.

II

Have you studied Arabic before?

Any prior experience — apps, classes, or self-study? Knowing what you have tried helps me teach better.

III

What is your ultimate goal?

Everyday conversation, reading the Quran, travel, or full fluency? Let us set a meaningful target together from the very start.

IV

How many hours per week can you dedicate?

Consistency is more valuable than intensity. Even thirty minutes a day makes a remarkable difference — I will build a plan around your life.

V

Do you prefer structure or conversation?

Some learners thrive with grammar and writing. Others want to speak from day one. Most enjoy a thoughtful blend — what suits you?

VI

What languages do you already speak?

Your linguistic background allows me to draw useful comparisons and explain Arabic in a way that resonates with you personally.
